We use high-quality materials for our katanas, including T10 steel, 1095 carbon steel, and folded steel. Each material is carefully selected to ensure durability, sharpness, and the traditional aesthetic of a well-crafted katana. We also offer katanas with clay-tempered blades, which provide an authentic hamon (temper line).

Our katanas are fully functional and designed for martial arts practice, such as tameshigiri (cutting practice). They are battle-ready, with sharp blades and robust construction.
